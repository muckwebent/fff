First Fruit Family Fund (FFF)

Program Overview

  • The Grants are jointly provided by members to assist fellow human beings.
  • Free involvement available at every location of our mission centers internationally.
  • Resulted from more than 15 years of investment, research, and training.
  • Believe in helping others and be willing to accept help from others.
  • Minimum required investment and willingness to invest it with a purpose.

Guidelines and Requirements

  • Investment maturity period using the power of imagination.
  • Power of creating wealth with unlimited people contributing to your wealth.
  • Assistance to break financial chains without harming others.
  • Channeling capital/grants for your investment to fulfill life dreams.

Program Policies

  • Form collection and submission process.
  • Strict confidentiality and non-transferability of forms.
  • Notification and identification/claim papers collection within specified timelines.
  • Investment commitment and forfeiture policy for non-compliance.

Additional Information

  • This program is not a lottery and does not have any spiritual implications or commitments other than assisting fellow human beings.
